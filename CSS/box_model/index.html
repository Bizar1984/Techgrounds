Block and inline- boxes.

Block boxes will break onto a new line.
The box will extend in the inline direction to fill the space
available in its container. In most cases this means
that the box will become as wide as its container,
filling up 100% of the space available.


Inline elements will not break onto a new line.
Width and height properties will not apply. Vertical padding, margin
and borders will apply but will not cause other
inline boxes to move away from the box. Horizontally it will...
